A PLACE TO BE FREE

This brand has been made in support of the LGBTQ+ community. There are many people in the world that want to bring their community together, but don't have the resources to make it happen. And there are those outside of the community that don't see the need for such spaces.

This identity gives you the building blocks that you need to show the world (and your preferred venues) why it's important. It gives you the tools to promote your events and bring people together.

Includes:

  • User Guide
  • Logo
  • Introduction Packs
  • Promotional materials
